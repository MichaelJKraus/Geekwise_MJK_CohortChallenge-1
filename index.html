UX/UI

Why is UX/UI important in site design?

To understand the importance of User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) design you must first understand that UX and UI are broad terms that encompass the overall user involvement while visiting a site. The primary goal of any business is to increase growth and sales of the business. A seamless user experience of your site is key to increases in both areas.

UX development is the process of improving users overall experience when they interact with a website or application and maximizing customer satisfaction. Keeping customers in mind when developing the website wireframing guides the consumer through the website in a logical and structured flow. This flow moves the user toward the target goal of purchasing products or services.

UI development, on the other hand, is the process of improving the interactions and presentation of a website or application. It focuses on the looks and interactions with the user and is responsive to user input. This includes the screen, buttons, navigation and other visual elements of a website.

With users having expansive choices in products and services, the objective it to grab the attention of the user in a short amount of time. First impressions are crucial, and last long. UX/UI design can make of break this impression and either drive customers toward you or away from you.

Core components of great UX/UI design are:

  • Usability: Is it easy to use?
  • Visual Design: What does it look like?
  • Wireframing: How does the prototype look?
  • Interaction Design: How do users engage with it?
  • Information Architecture: Does the structure/flow make sense?

Since UX and UI development largely focus on the users' satisfaction and pleasure in website use, it plays a crucial role in business success. The user satisfaction is essential in building the brand, value and reputation of the business. UX and UI design services from LawDesign can build these key elements for your law firm's success.
