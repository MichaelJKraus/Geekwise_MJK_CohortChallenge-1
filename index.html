  • 7/31/2019

    Today we finished off the "Restaurante" page before the first break. After the break we began the process of making adjustments to the final projects. It is required we have the following elements incorporated into our pages.Readable code, good file structure, nested code, all links work, bootstrap formatting, responsive site and a media query. We also must heave a GitHub Repo, Navbar, Accordion or thumbnails, at least three pages of content, link to a site I created, at least 4 social media icons, 2 Google Fonts and a contact form. Tonight we crammed out the code and had the teacher available for questions and suggestions. Because I was able to work ahead during the week, I have most of the requirements added already. Another good night!
